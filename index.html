Usage
Enter Variables separated by commas and no spaces
Enter expression or multiple expressions separated by commands and no spaces
AND {expression} && {expression}
OR {expression} || {expression}
NOT !{expression}
NAND {expression} (&) {expression}
NOR {expression} (|) {expression}
XOR {expression} (+) {expression}

Note NAND, NOR, XOR is not currently supported
